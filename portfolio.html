College

A College is seen as a stepping stone to a sound career. But a degree alone doesn't equip one to become an exceptional professional. That is why our approach at St. Mary's has always been holistic. Education here means - academics coupled with a conducive and friendly environment, new-age technology.

Interaction

The management, then contemplated on offering higher education to the students in a better atmosphere and with the state of the art infrastructure. There was a vision and it made its beginning in 2002 when a beautiful, state of art college building emerged in Yousufguda, reflecting the dreams of the management.
